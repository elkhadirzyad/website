A cyber network attack detection based on GM Median Nearest Neighbors LDA

In this paper we ameliorate the performance of a feature extraction method called median NN-LDA, we replace the arithmetic means by general means which are more resistant to outliers. As a consequence, the approach gives promising results in term of network intrusion detection.
Elkhadir Zyad, Benattou Mohamed.
Computers & Security, 2019

Network Intrusion Detection System Using PCA by Lp-Norm Maximization Based on Conjugate Gradient

In this work we prove that using conjugate gradient yields good results in term of PCA-lp minimisation. The approach is faster, optimal and more accurate than the gradient descent, on the other hand, it gives better DR on datasets KDDcup99 and NSL-KDD.
Elkhadir Zyad, Chougdali Khalid, Benattou Mohamed
International Review on Computers and Software, 2016

Exploiting scene constraints to improve object detection algorithms for industrial applications

In this PhD dissertation we explore the possibility to use scene-specific constraints of industrial object detection tasks to influence three main aspects of object detection algorithms: the amount of training data, the speed of the detection process and the amount of FP/FN detections. Moreover, we propose steps to simplify the training process using techniques like active learning and data augmentation.
Steven Puttemans
Doctoral Manuscript,2017
